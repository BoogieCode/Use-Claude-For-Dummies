### The Complete Beginner's Guide

A friendly, jargon-free handbook for getting real work out of
[Claude](https://claude.ai), Anthropic's AI assistant — from your
first click to power-user techniques.

## What's inside

**Part I — Meet Your New Assistant (Chapters 1–12)**
What Claude is and how LLMs work (in plain English), setting up your
account, your first conversations, every major feature explained,
free vs. paid plans, what Claude gets wrong (hallucinations, context
windows), safety and privacy, 20 copy-paste recipes, troubleshooting,
and a glossary.

**Part II — Actually Getting Good (Chapters 13–23)**
The efficiency handbook: the anatomy of a power prompt, the iteration
loop, pro techniques, conversation hygiene, Projects, a reusable
template library, workflows that multiply your output, the seven
deadly sins of prompting, and a printable one-page cheat sheet.

## Who it's for

Complete beginners. No tech background needed — short chapters,
concrete examples, diagrams for the tricky concepts, and
Tip / Warning / Remember callouts throughout.
